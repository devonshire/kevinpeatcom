CNC Z-Axis Build

I had a lot of trouble with the plate that sits between the Y-axis and the Z-axis. This required 40 holes to be drilled very accurately in an aluminium plate which I found very difficult to do with the tools available. A drill with laser alignment would have helped greatly in this regard.
