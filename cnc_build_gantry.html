CNC Machine DIY Build - Gantry

Putting together the gantry for my CNC mill. The key here is that the gantry must be square which in this case I checked by carefully measuring that the diagonals are the same length.

The sides of the gantry being aluminium box section they are not super-rigid but I think they will be strong enough for the relatively soft materials that I would like to machine. I plan to replace them in the future with aluminium plate when time allows.
